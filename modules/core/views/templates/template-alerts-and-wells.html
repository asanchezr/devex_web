Alerts & Wells

Alerts

Alert should be used for:

  • Alerting the user to an action they need to take on the page, or
  • Giving the user confirmation or feedback on an action they've just taken

Wells

Wells are like alerts, just fatter. Use wells sparingly to:

  • Separate out standalone bits of content that don't require any specific action by the user, and
  • Are not for the purpose of providing immediate feedback on an action just taken.
